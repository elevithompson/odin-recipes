Spicy Veggie Pasta

Description

A creamy and spicy veggie pasta crafted from a simple process

Ingredients

  • 6 oz spinach
  • Olive Oil
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • Garlic (Minced)
  • 3/4 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1 tbsp italian seasoning
  • Chipotle Chile Pepper
  • Salt / Black Pepper
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Parmesan cheese or Parmesan Reggiano
  • Pasta of choice

Steps

  1. Add gartlic and onions to pan with olive oil, cook until onions turn a bit red
  2. Add 2tbsp lemon juice, 1tbsp italian seasoning, sprinkle black pepper, 1 tbsp Chipotle Chile Pepeper then add spinach to wilt
  3. Add heavy whipping cream and tomatoes, simmer until whipping cream starts to thicken
  4. Add pasta and mix
